Chen Chen

Biography

Chen is currently a third year PhD student with Computer Science department at Loughborough University, UK.
His research interests are broadly in areas of Software Defined Network, Cloud/Edge computing, network resource orchestration, in-network computing, distributed system, mobile networks, kubernetes and P4.
Prior to joining Loughborough Univeristy in 2019, he received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Xidian University, Xi’an, China and M.S degree in Electrical Engineering from TU Dresden, Germany.
After that he worked in the industry (e.g., Infineon and Huawei Technology) for a few years.
