Chen Chen

Biography

Chen is a postdoc at Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Cambridge.
His research interests are broadly in areas of IoT, Serverless Computing, Cloud/Edge Computing, Network Resource Orchestration, In-network Computing, Distributed System and Mobile Networks. His latest work focus on resource orchestration in serverless edge computing, aiming to optimize the overall performance for the system, including latency, energy consumption and system cost.
